Migration in the Sea

Some fly 11 days nonstop. Others trek 8,000 miles. Each year, thousands of bird species leave home in search of food. Every spring and fall, a spectacle unfolds in the night sky as millions of birds attempt long, perilous journeys between their summer breeding and wintering grounds. Most of the thousands of bird species that engage in this annual migration travel at night, when wind currents are smoother and the moon and stars guide their way.
